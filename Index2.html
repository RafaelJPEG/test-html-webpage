potato trivia:
  1. Potatoes in Space
    • The first potato grown in space was part of NASA's "Veggie" experiment in 2015.
    • The goal was to see if potatoes could grow in microgravity and support long-term space missions.

  2. The World’s Largest Potato
    • The largest potato ever recorded weighed a whopping 18 pounds 4 ounces (8.2 kg).
    • It was found in the UK in 2011 and was named “The Big Potato.”

  3. Potatoes as Currency:
    • In 19th-century Ireland, potatoes were sometimes used as a form of payment during hard times.
    • Potatoes were also used to pay workers during the Great Irish Potato Famine.
